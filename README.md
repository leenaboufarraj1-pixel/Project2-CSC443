BowRun: Wave-Based Survival Shooter



Project Overview

The player must survive increasingly difficult waves of animal enemies in a forest arena, managing points to buy ammo, health, and weapon upgrades between rounds.



Controls Movement: 

WASD Keys 

Aim/Look: Mouse Move 

Shoot: Left Click 

Open/Close Shop: you enter a tent. 

Switch Weapons: buy from shop

Enemy Types: Dog, Dear, Tiger with different attach ranges, attack damage, attack rate and speed



Upgrades \& Weapons Ammo Refill: 

Replenishes arrows for your current weapon.

&#x20;Health Recovery: Restores 25 HP to help survive the next wave. 

Long Bow: Higher range and damage than the starting bow.

Crossbow: Rapid-fire capabilities for clearing large groups of enemies.



Polish Features:

Damage Tint: A red flash on the HUD indicates when the player is hit.

Floating UI: The Shop label bobs up and down and always faces the player for better visibility.

Dynamic Health Bar: The health bar changes color (Green → Yellow → Red) as health drops.

Animals Animation: walk and idle animations for enemies.
